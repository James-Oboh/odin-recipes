Potato Salad Recipe

Description

This creamy potato salad is a classic dish perfect for picnics and barbecues. Made with tender potatoes, crisp celery, and a tangy dressing, itâ€™s a crowd-pleaser that can be made ahead of time.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of potatoes
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of mustard
  • 1/2 cup of chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes in salted water until tender, about 15-20 minutes. Drain and let cool.
  2. Once cooled, peel and chop the potatoes into bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise, mustard, celery, onion, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add the chopped potatoes to the bowl and gently mix until well coated.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley before serving.
