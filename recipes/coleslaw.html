Coleslaw Recipe

Description

Coleslaw is a classic salad made primarily from finely shredded raw cabbage and dressed most commonly with a vinaigrette or mayonnaise. It's a refreshing side dish that pairs well with barbecued meats and sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 1 small head of green cabbage, shredded
  • 2 large carrots, grated
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage and grated carrots.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cabbage and carrots, and toss until well combined.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
