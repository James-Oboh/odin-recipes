Macaroni Salad Recipe

Description

This macaroni salad is a classic dish that combines tender macaroni pasta with a creamy dressing, fresh vegetables, and a hint of tanginess. It's perfect for picnics, barbecues, or as a side dish for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and apple cider vinegar (if using).
  3. Add the cooked macaroni, celery, bell pepper, red onion, and pickle relish to the bowl. Mix well to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the dressing as needed.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
